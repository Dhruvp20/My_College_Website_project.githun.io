ABOUT THE INSTITUTE

Rajkiya Engineering College Banda (Formerly Dr. Bhim Rao Ambedkar Engineering College of Information Technology: BRAECIT) was established by the Government of Uttar Pradesh in the year 2010 with three branches Electrical Engineering (EE), Mechanical Engineering (ME) and Information Technology (IT) with annual intake of Sixty (60) in each branch. The college is affiliated to Dr A P J Abdul Kalam Technical University, Lucknow (Formerly Uttar Pradesh Technical University, Lucknow). The college has moved to its own fully residential campus located at Atarra, Banda in the month of July 2015 and all academic activities from the session 2015-16 are being carried out at its own campus. Fully residential campus of college consists of Academic Building, Workshop, Five Hostels (Boys and Girls), Residential Apartments, Student Activity Centre, Gymnasium, Sport facilities, Library, Canteen, Grocery Store and other facilities for recreation.
The College has fully air-conditioned Smart Class Rooms equipped with modern teaching aid, such as, Touch Screen Podium, Interactive White Boards, Short Through Projector along with Audio Visual facilities. The college also has 500 KVA generator unit capable of providing electricity to whole campus. Students are provided opportunities to go to institutes of national importance like IITs/NITs/NIE Mysuru for training and workshops. Employability Skill training is given to third year students in evening hours to improve their communication skills and overall personality.
The college is situated on the Banda-Chitrakoot Road approximately 28 km from Banda and 05 km from Atarra. Both Banda and Atarra is well connected by train and road with direct train connectivity to major cities, such as, New Delhi, Lucknow, Kanpur, Jhansi, Allahabad and Varanasi.
